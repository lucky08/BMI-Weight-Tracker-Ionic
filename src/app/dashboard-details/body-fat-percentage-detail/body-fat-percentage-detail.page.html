Body Fat Percentage Calculator
Body fat percentage refers to the proportion of fat tissue in the body compared to total body weight. It is expressed as a percentage and includes both essential fat, necessary for normal physiological functions, and storage fat, which is used as an energy reserve.

Body Fat Percentage Calculation Formula

The formula developed by Deurenberg et al. (1991) is commonly used:

For Adults:

Body Fat Percentage=(1.20×BMI)+(0.23×Age)−(10.8×Gender Factor)−5.4

For Teenagers (Children and Adolescents aged 5–18):

Body Fat Percentage=(1.51×BMI)−(0.70×Age)−(3.6×Gender Factor)+1.4

Gender Factor:

Use 1 for males

Use 0 for females
