BMI Calculator
Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value derived from an individual's weight and height, commonly used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight relative to their height. It provides a simple, standardized way to categorize weight status and can indicate whether someone is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Calculation Formula

The formula for calculating BMI is:

Metric:

BMI = Weight (kg) / [Height (m)]²

Imperial:

BMI = [Weight (lbs) × 703] / [Height (inches)]²
